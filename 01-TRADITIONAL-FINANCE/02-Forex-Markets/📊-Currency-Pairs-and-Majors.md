## ğŸ’± Apa itu Currency Pairs?

**Currency Pairs** adalah pasangan mata uang yang diperdagangkan di pasar forex, dimana satu mata uang dibeli dan mata uang lainnya dijual. Nilainya menunjukkan berapa banyak unit mata uang quote yang dibutuhkan untuk membeli satu unit mata uang base.

### ğŸ¯ Struktur Dasar
```markdown
**Format: BASE/QUOTE**

- **Base Currency**: Mata uang yang dibeli (pertama)
- **Quote Currency**: Mata uang yang dijual (kedua)
- **Exchange Rate**: Harga satu unit base dalam quote currency

**Contoh:**
- **EUR/USD = 1.0800** â†’ 1 Euro = 1.08 US Dollar
- **USD/JPY = 149.50** â†’ 1 US Dollar = 149.50 Yen
- **GBP/JPY = 190.25** â†’ 1 British Pound = 190.25 Yen
```

### ğŸ“Š Klasifikasi Currency Pairs
```markdown
**1. Major Pairs (7 pairs)**
- Melibatkan USD + mata uang utama lainnya
- 70% volume trading global
- Liquidity tinggi, spread rendah

**2. Minor Pairs (Crosses)**
- Tidak melibatkan USD
- Mata uang utama vs mata uang utama
- Liquidity sedang, spread sedang

**3. Exotic Pairs**
- Mata uang utama vs emerging market
- Liquidity rendah, spread tinggi
- Volatilitas tinggi

**4. Regional Pairs**
- Pasangan dalam satu wilayah
- Contoh: EUR/GBP, AUD/NZD
```

## ğŸ¯ Trading Strategies

### ğŸ“ˆ Trend Following Strategies
```markdown
**1. Multi-Timeframe Analysis**
- **Daily**: Trend direction identification
- **H4**: Entry timing, pullback opportunities
- **H1**: Entry execution, stop loss placement
- **M15**: Fine-tuning entries, exits

**2. Moving Average Systems**
- **EMAs**: 20, 50, 200 for trend identification
- **Signal**: Fast MA crosses slow MA
- **Confirmation**: Volume, price action
- **Risk**: 1-2% per trade, 3:1 reward ratio

**3. Breakout Trading**
- **Setup**: Range consolidation, support/resistance
- **Entry**: Break with volume confirmation
- **Stop**: Below/above breakout level
- **Target**: Measured move, next resistance
```

### ğŸ“Š Range Trading Strategies
```markdown
**1. Support/Resistance Bounce**
- **Setup**: Clear horizontal levels
- **Entry**: Bounce from S/R with confirmation
- **Stop**: Beyond S/R level
- **Target**: Opposite side of range

**2. Oscillator Trading**
- **RSI**: Overbought/oversold levels (70/30)
- **Stochastic**: Similar to RSI but smoother
- **MACD**: Divergence, crossover signals
- **Confirmation**: Price action, volume

**3. Asian Session Range**
- **Setup**: Asian session consolidation
- **Strategy**: Fade extremes, range boundaries
- **Risk**: Low volatility, small targets
- **Session**: Focus on Tokyo/Sydney overlap
```

### ğŸ”„ Carry Trade Strategies
```markdown
**1. Classic Carry Trade**
- **Long**: High-yield currency (AUD, NZD)
- **Short**: Low-yield currency (JPY, CHF)
- **Income**: Interest rate differential
- **Risk**: Currency depreciation

**2. Hedged Carry Trade**
- **Strategy**: Partial forward hedging
- **Benefits**: Reduce currency risk
- **Cost**: Lower interest income
- **Suitable**: Conservative investors

**3. Emerging Market Carry**
- **Long**: High-yield EM currencies (BRL, TRY, IDR)
- **Short**: Low-yield developed currencies
- **Risk**: Political, economic instability
- **Reward**: Very high interest differentials
```

## ğŸ›¡ï¸ Risk Management

### âš ï¸ Forex Trading Risks
```markdown
**1. Market Risk**
- **Volatility**: 100-500 pip daily moves possible
- **Gap Risk**: Weekend/holiday gaps
- **Liquidity Risk**: Slippage during news events
- **Black Swan Events**: Extreme market movements

**2. Leverage Risk**
- **Standard Leverage**: 50:1 to 500:1 retail
- **Professional**: Up to 30:1 in EU, 50:1 elsewhere
- **Risk**: Total account loss possible
- **Management**: Never risk >2% per trade

**3. Currency Risk**
- **Exchange Rate Risk**: Currency value changes
- **Correlation Risk**: Multiple positions correlated
- **Country Risk**: Political/economic instability
- **Settlement Risk**: Counterparty default
```

### ğŸ›¡ï¸ Risk Management Techniques
```markdown
**Position Sizing:**
- **Risk per Trade**: 1-2% of account balance
- **Stop Loss**: Technical + volatility based
- **Maximum Exposure**: 10% total account risk
- **Correlation Limits**: 5% per currency group

**Stop Loss Strategies:**
- **Technical Stops**: Below support/above resistance
- **Volatility Stops**: ATR-based (1.5-2x ATR)
- **Time Stops**: Exit if trade doesn't work
- **Equity Stops**: Fixed percentage loss

**Portfolio Management:**
- **Currency Diversification**: Multiple currencies
- **Strategy Diversification**: Different timeframes
- **Correlation Monitoring**: Avoid overexposure
- **Regular Rebalancing**: Weekly/monthly review
```
