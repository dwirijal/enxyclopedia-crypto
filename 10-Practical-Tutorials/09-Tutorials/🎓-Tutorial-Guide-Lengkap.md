#### **Stop Loss dan Take Profit**
```
üõ°Ô∏è Risk Management dengan Stop Orders:

Stop Loss Order:
1. Klik tab "Stop"
2. Pilih "Stop Loss"
3. Masukkan stop price (contoh: $2,200)
4. Masukkan limit price (biasanya sedikit di bawah stop)
5. Jika harga turun ke stop price, otomatis jual
6. Berguna untuk limit loss

Take Profit Order:
1. Klik tab "Stop"
2. Pilih "Take Profit"
3. Masukkan take profit price (contoh: $2,900)
4. Masukkan limit price
5. Jika harga naik ke target, otomatis jual
6. Lock in profit

OCO (One Cancels Other):
1. Set stop loss dan take profit bersamaan
2. Jika salah satu ter-execute, yang lain cancel
3. Automate risk management
4. Cocok untuk swing trading
```

### **Tutorial 5: Technical Analysis Dasar**

#### **Meng Candlestick Patterns**
```
üïØÔ∏è Candlestick Analysis:

Candlestick Components:
- **Body**: Harga open dan close
- **Wicks/Shadows**: High dan low prices
- **Green/White**: Bullish (close > open)
- **Red/Black**: Bearish (close < open)

Common Patterns:
1. **Doji**: Indecision, open ‚âà close
2. **Hammer**: Reversal signal, long lower wick
3. **Shooting Star**: Reversal signal, long upper wick
4. **Engulfing**: Strong reversal, body engulfs previous
5. **Morning Star**: Bullish reversal, 3-candle pattern
6. **Evening Star**: Bearish reversal, 3-candle pattern

Timeframes:
- 1m, 5m, 15m: Scalping
- 1h, 4h: Day trading
- 1D: Swing trading
- 1W, 1M: Long-term analysis
```

#### **Support dan Resistance**
```
üìä Support and Resistance Levels:

Support Level:
- Price di mana buying interest meningkat
- Bisa jadi floor untuk harga
- Area where price tends to bounce
- Bisa ditarik dari:
  - Previous lows
  - Psychological levels (round numbers)
  - Trend lines
  - Moving averages

Resistance Level:
- Price di mana selling pressure meningkat
- Bisa jadi ceiling untuk harga
- Area where price tends to reverse
- Bisa ditarik dari:
  - Previous highs
  - Psychological levels
  - Trend lines
  - Moving averages

How to Draw:
1. Connect previous swing highs/lows
2. Look for horizontal price levels
3. Identify psychological levels ($20k, $30k, etc.)
4. Use multiple touches for confirmation
5. Adjust as market evolves

Trading with S/R:
- Buy near support, sell near resistance
- Breakout above resistance = bullish
- Breakdown below support = bearish
- Support broken becomes resistance
- Resistance broken becomes support
```

#### **Basic Indicators**
```
üìà Technical Indicators:

Moving Averages (MA):
- **SMA**: Simple Moving Average
- **EMA**: Exponential Moving Average
- **WMA**: Weighted Moving Average

Common Periods:
- MA 20: Short-term trend
- MA 50: Medium-term trend
- MA 200: Long-term trend

Usage:
- Price above MA = bullish
- Price below MA = bearish
- MA crossover signals
- Dynamic support/resistance

Relative Strength Index (RSI):
- Oscillator antara 0-100
- Overbought: RSI > 70
- Oversold: RSI < 30
- Divergence signals
- Momentum indicator

MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence):
- MACD line and signal line
- Histogram shows momentum
- Crossover signals
- Divergence analysis
- Trend confirmation

Volume:
- Confirms price movements
- High volume = strong conviction
- Low volume = weak conviction
- Volume breakout patterns
- Accumulation/distribution
```

#### **Position Sizing**
```
üìè Position Sizing Calculator:

Risk Management Formula:
- Risk per trade = 1-2% of portfolio
- Position size = Risk Amount / (Entry - Stop Loss)
- Always use stop loss
- Adjust based on volatility

Example:
- Portfolio: $10,000
- Risk per trade: 1% = $100
- Entry: $50,000
- Stop Loss: $48,000
- Risk per unit: $2,000
- Position size: $100 / $2,000 = 0.05 BTC

Volatility Adjustment:
- High volatility = smaller position
- Low volatility = larger position
- Use ATR for volatility measurement
- Adjust stop loss distance
- Consider correlation with portfolio
```

```
üí∏ Crypto Tax Basics:

Taxable Events:
1. **Selling Crypto for Fiat**
   - Realized capital gain/loss
   - Short-term (<1 year) or long-term (>1 year)
   - Report on tax return

2. **Trading Crypto for Crypto**
   - Each trade is taxable
   - Calculate gain/loss in fiat terms
   - Keep detailed records

3. **Using Crypto for Purchases**
   - Treated as sale event
   - Calculate gain/loss
   - Report accordingly

4. **Mining/Staking Rewards**
   - Taxed as income when received
   - Cost basis for future sales
   - Self-employment tax possible

Tax Calculation:
- **Capital Gain**: Sale Price - Cost Basis
- **Short-term**: Ordinary income rates
- **Long-term**: Preferential rates (0%, 15%, 20%)
- **Wash Sale Rules**: May apply
- **Like-Kind Exchange**: No longer applies

Record Keeping:
- Buy/sell dates
- Purchase prices
- Sale prices
- Fees paid
- Transfer records
- Wallet addresses

#### **Cara Calculate dan Report Crypto Tax**
```
üìä Tax Calculation Process:

Step-by-Step Tax Calculation:
1. **Gather All Transactions**:
   - Exchange statements
   - Wallet transaction history
   - DeFi activity records
   - NFT sales/purchases
   - Mining/staking rewards

2. **Calculate Cost Basis**:
   - FIFO (First In, First Out)
   - LIFO (Last In, First Out)
   - Specific Identification
   - Average Cost Basis
   - Choose method and stick to it

3. **Calculate Gains/Losses**:
   ```
   Gain = Sale Price - Cost Basis
   Loss = Cost Basis - Sale Price
   ```

4. **Classify as Short or Long-term**:
   - Short-term: Held 1 year or less
   - Long-term: Held more than 1 year
   - Different tax rates apply

5. **Apply Tax Rates**:
   - Short-term: Ordinary income rates
   - Long-term: 0%, 15%, or 20%
   - State taxes may apply
   - Net Investment Income Tax (3.8%)
